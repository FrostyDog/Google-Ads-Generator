FAQ

Who needs Ad Generators?

This generator was made for both Novie advertisers and marketing experts:

    Novice advertisers, who have no previous experience with writting any kind of text ads for "Google Ads". Ads Generator would give you a fast and decent start. Because most of the important tasks are already done, and you can concentrate on more important tasks with your bussiness.
    Experienced marketers and agencies, could have a significant boost of their workflow, no matter how many projects, products or locations you have.

This Generator allows you to builds skeleton of Ads dedicated to your company. Generated ads suppose to give you some general ideas, so you can than add your very specific details, and launch a good quality Ads.

I see only generator for Google Ads. More coming?

For now concentration is on Google Ads. It is highly possible that in the future more generators for other platform would come up.

Ads have same information?

Ads would have some simular information in them, but it is highly unlikely that you would have exactly the same ad.

Would same information affect my quality score?

No, read more about Quality Score on Google Support

Why do I need different "Ad group" and "Campaign" generated

Digital marketing is all about testing and looking what works or not. Since Ads have simular but not exactly 100% same info, after some time of testing, you can easily tell what is working better for you, and what is not. Making a structure in you account - helps to do such a thing
